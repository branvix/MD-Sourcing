Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

MOQs vary by supplier and product category. We work with you to find suppliers that can accommodate your volume requirements, even negotiating lower MOQs when possible. For many product categories, we can arrange mixed orders to help you meet minimum requirements.

How do you ensure product quality?

We implement a comprehensive quality control process that includes supplier verification, pre-production samples, during-production inspections, and final quality checks before shipping. Our local quality control teams in China, Thailand, and Indonesia conduct thorough inspections based on your specifications.

What are your typical lead times?

Lead times depend on product complexity, order volume, and supplier availability. Typically, production takes 20-60 days after order confirmation, and shipping time varies based on destination and shipping method. We provide detailed timelines for each project and keep you updated throughout the process.

Can you source custom-designed products?

Yes, we specialize in custom product sourcing. We work with manufacturers who can produce according to your specifications and help develop samples for your approval before mass production. Our team can also assist with design refinements to optimize for manufacturing efficiency and cost.
